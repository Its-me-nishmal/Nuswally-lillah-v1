Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Nuswally Lillah (referred to as "the App") is an offline-first Islamic companion application developed by Nishmal Vadakara. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe, secure user experience.

🛡️ Privacy First Commitment: Nuswally Lillah is built as an offline-first utility. We do not collect, store, track, or share any of your personal data, location data, or usage metrics.

1. Information Collection and Use

Because the App is designed to run entirely locally on your device, our data policy is simple:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or device identifiers.
  • Location Data: The App calculates prayer times using astronomical formulas based on your selected coordinates. This location configuration is stored entirely locally on your device's secure storage and is never transmitted to any external servers.
  • No Third-Party Analytics/Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics software (like Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.) or tracking tools within the App.

2. Data Storage

All settings, Quran bookmarks, Tasbeeh counts, and custom alarm configurations are saved directly to your device's local memory using secure, standard storage protocols. If you delete the App, this data is completely removed from your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not contain links to external third-party websites, nor does it display advertisements (such as Google AdMob). All calculations and audios are served offline directly from your device package.

4. Children's Privacy

Since the App does not collect any personal information, it is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations, including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR. It is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
